Experiences

Internship: Kelantan State Museum

During my internship at the Kelantan State Museum, I gained hands-on experience in preserving and promoting cultural heritage. My responsibilities included assisting with the curation of exhibits, organizing historical artifacts, and contributing to visitor engagement activities.

I worked closely with the museum team to design educational materials and enhance visitor experiences by providing detailed explanations of the exhibits' significance. This experience helped me develop strong organizational, communication, and research skills while deepening my appreciation for Kelantan's rich history and culture.
